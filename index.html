About Me

I did a general computing and electronics courses at Shenyang Aerospace University in China where I started to learn C languange and assembly language and also learned embeded system hardware design. That’s where my interest in programming. When I moved to Canada 5 years ago, I took networking and computer classes at MITT where I understanding how it works from hardware level to TCP/ IP protocol.


Recently I went to York University. The certificate they had to offer were really great for me because I could tailor my course to fit what I wanted to do (front-end and back-end). I could concentrate on the modules that would help strengthen my skills (for instance Web Scripting, Web App Development, Programming), without having to worry about classes that weren’t as relevant to what I wanted to do.


I worked at a company for about 2.5 year after graduating from MITT. I saw they were looking for a new Front-End techinical support and I really liked the look of the company, so I went for it! Since I started I’ve been working on a variety of different projects, constantly learning new tools and different techniques. It’s challenging work and really rewarding.


I have a passion on web design as well. Maybe because I am very sensive for colors & patterns, and love painting since I was 2 years old. I feel this is a huge asset for taking web development position.
